Weather Site Plan

Purpose Statement

This site is to inform visitors of the weather forecast in Franklin, ID, Greenville, SC, and SpringField, IL. It will include information concerning high and low temperatures, precipitation chance, wind speed and direction, as well as weather on the hour. This information will assist visitors in their daily planning, as they will know what the weather will be like.

Audience Description

The audience of this site will include both men and women that are concerned about the weather. These people love to discuss the weather with others, so they always want to be up to date with conditions. If these individuals had more free time, they would spend it at the window, watching the sky. They all grew up with the dream to be a meteorologist.
